# Veklom Positioning - Source of Truth

**Veklom is authority infrastructure for agent action.**

Veklom decides what AI agents are allowed to do before they do it -
and verifies whether the systems they call are trustworthy enough
to receive the action.

## Architecture

Cappo authorizes the action.
VNP grades the capability.
BYOS runs the workload.
Control Plane governs the system.

## This repo

This repo implements one layer of the Veklom Runtime Authority Stack.

## The one sentence

Veklom is not just controlling agents.
Veklom is controlling the relationship between agents and the
systems they touch.
